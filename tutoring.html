/tutoring

Need help with math or physics? Let's work together! I am comfortable working with any student middle school and up.

The cumulative nature of these subjects can feel overwhelming. Too often students get left behind, and as material builds, understanding can feel impossible.

Whether you're starting Algebra for the first time, are rebuilding a foundation to take on a more complex subject, or just need a bit of assistance with a single assignment, I'm here to help.

I'm experienced in working with & developing strategies for students with ADHD and test anxiety, and can help students establish and celebrate acheivable goals.

I am available for online sessions, or in-person depending on your location.
